What is VEX?

  • Home/
  • VEX/
  • What is VEX?

VEX is a STEM and Technology based company that aims to bring students together to create robots in which they can build, program and document.
Each year, VEX creates a new game for students to focus on. Students try to score as many points as they can in the 15 second autonomous period, and 1 minute 45 second driver control period in qualification matches, elimination matches, and skills challenge.
Students must continually use the Engineering Design Process because VEX releases a new game each year.
Vex Robotics is located in over 60 countries and serves over 1,000,000 students.
